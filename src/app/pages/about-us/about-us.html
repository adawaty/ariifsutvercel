What is
ARIIF?

ARIIF is an annual undergraduate research forum that brings together students, academia, and industry to showcase applied research, foster innovation, and address real-world challenges.

Held during the second semester of each academic year at the ElSewedy University of Technology (SUTECH) Campus, the forum is a flagship initiative reflecting the university’s commitment to learning through innovation and practice-oriented research.

The competition features two distinct pathways: course-based projects from various academic years and independent research projects.

This guide provides comprehensive information on the competition structure, submission guidelines, timeline, and prizes to ensure your successful participation.

Objectives

  • 01. Promote a culture of research and innovation
  • 02. Encourage interdisciplinary collaboration
  • 03. Enhance presentation and communication skills
  • 04. Connect students with academic and industry experts

ARIIF is guided by the principles of

Innovation, Academic Integrity, Teamwork, and Real-World Impact.
