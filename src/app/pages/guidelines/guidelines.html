Guidelines & Playbook

How to
Participate

Follow these three stages to secure your spot at the ARIIF 2026 exhibition. Prepare your deliverables carefully.

Stage 01

Prepare Your Project Materials

  • Secure Supervisor Approval: Download and sign the template.
  • Two-Page Summary: Outline goals, methodology, and results (PDF).
  • Project Video: Record a 3–4 minute pitch of your idea.
Approval Template
Course Template Research Template

Stage 02

Submit Final Deliverables

Once you receive notification of your qualification, prepare and upload your final project assets. Incomplete submissions will not be accepted.

  • Technical Document: A detailed report of your findings and development process.
  • Prototype/Demo: A functional model or recorded demonstration of your solution.
Upload to Portal

Stage 03

Showcase at ARIIF

The Grand Stage. Present your hardware or software implementations in a professional exhibition setting.

  • Location: ElSewedy University of Technology (SUTECH) Campus.
  • When: The week of April 19, 2026.
  • Compete: Defend your work in front of industry leaders and professors.
