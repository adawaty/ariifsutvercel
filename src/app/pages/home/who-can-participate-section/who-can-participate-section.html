Eligibility & Criteria

Who Can
Participate?

ARIIF is an exclusive platform dedicated to showcasing the brightest minds at SUTECH. Ensure you meet the following criteria to join the competition.

  • Undergraduate Students

    Open exclusively to SUTECH undergraduates.

  • Individuals or Teams

    Participate solo or collaborate with your peers to build something extraordinary.

  • QMS Upload Required

    All Project Profiles must be successfully uploaded to the University Quality Management System (QMS).

  • Supervisor Approval

    You must obtain formal approval from your course supervisor before registering.
