Evaluation Metrics

Judging Criteria

How top-tier industry experts and academic professors will evaluate your project.

01

Innovation & Creativity

The originality of the idea and the uniqueness of the proposed solution.

02

Practical & Industrial Impact

The potential for real-world application and the project's ability to solve specific industry challenges.

03

Quality & Implementation

The rigor of the methodology, the stability of the Prototype, and the depth of the Technical Document.

04

Communication Skills

The clarity of the Project Video, visual quality of the Poster, and the team's ability to engage with the jury.

⭐ Focus on projects that solve real problems, are feasible, and clearly presented.
